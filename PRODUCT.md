# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Users who want to stream or download TeraBox links directly in the browser without being forced to download an app.

## Product Purpose
A fast, stealthy utility to bypass app blocks and handle TeraBox links seamlessly.

## Brand Personality
Stark, high-contrast, premium, brutalist-editorial, no-nonsense.

## Anti-references
Typical bubbly SaaS, soft pastel themes, playful illustrations, generic bootstrap dashboards.

## Design Principles
- Function over decoration
- Stark contrast for focus
- Unapologetic utility

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA contrast minimums. Focus outlines must be stark and highly visible.
